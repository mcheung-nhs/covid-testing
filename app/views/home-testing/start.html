Register a home test kit

You can register a coronavirus (COVID-19) test for you or someone else

Check if you can register a home test

You should only register a home test kit if:

  • you've already received it
  • you’ve already booked a courier to collect your test sample

To complete this form, you’ll need

  • the test kit you're registering
  • your email address, or an email address for the person you're registering it for
  • your mobile number, or a mobile number for the person you're registering it for

If people you live with have received a test kit

  • you should register their kits, too (you can register up to 4 other kits once you’ve registered your own)
  • it is very important that each person takes the test registered to them to make sure they receive the correct results

How we use your data
